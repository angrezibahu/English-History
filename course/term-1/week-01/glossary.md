## Week 1 — Glossary

- **Sub-Roman Britain** — the period from roughly 410 to 550, after imperial rule
  but before stable Anglo-Saxon kingdoms.
- **Foederati** — barbarian troops settled inside the empire to defend it; some
  Saxons may have first arrived this way.
- **Adventus Saxonum** — Latin for "the coming of the Saxons", the traditional
  name for the migration.
- **Bretwalda** — a later term for an overlord recognised across several kingdoms.
